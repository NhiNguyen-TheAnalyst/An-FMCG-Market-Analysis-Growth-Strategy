# Accelerate with Traditional Trade: An FMCG Market Analysis & Growth Strategy

A consumer goods market analysis project for **Action**, the #2 player in Vietnam's laundry detergent category, evaluating how to close the gap with market leader **Big** and decide on its next investment move.

## Business Context

Action is a Japanese laundry brand sold in Vietnam via exports from Thailand. What started as a small, entrepreneur-led import business in the early 2010s grew quickly enough that Action took direct control of distribution in 2013, then expanded into Modern Trade in 2015. The brand now faces a strategic fork: **invest in domestic manufacturing, or continue distributing from Thailand?**

That decision hinges on one question: **how much further can Action realistically grow in Vietnam?**. This project answers it by analyzing three years of retail audit data (2013–2015) to size the opportunity, diagnose the market leader's vulnerabilities, and recommend where Action should focus its growth investment.

## Objective

1. Assess the size and prospect of the overall laundry category in Vietnam
2. Identify Big's (the market leader's) strengths and weaknesses
3. Pinpoint Action's best growth opportunities by **region, channel, and product**
4. Recommend a go-to-market plan (place, product, price, promotion)
5. Forecast revenue impact for 2016

| File | Description |

## Methodology

- **Category health check**: tracked total category value sales (2013–2015) against macro indicators (GDP, Consumer Confidence Index) to test for saturation
- **Competitive share analysis**: mapped value % share shifts for all 5 brands across regions and channels (Q1'13 vs. Q4'15) to find where the leader is losing ground
- **Channel & regional decomposition**: broke down Traditional Trade Rural (the category's largest channel) into 7 sub-regions to find where Action's growth is concentrated vs. underdeveloped
- **Price-position benchmarking**: compared average price indices (vs. category) across brands and pack sizes to identify pricing white space
- **Promotion ROI read**: linked promotional intensity to volume growth by pack size to flag which growth is structural vs. promo-dependent
- **Revenue modeling**: built a forward forecast combining organic portfolio growth with a new product launch

## Key Findings

**Category**
- The laundry category is essentially flat (−0.025% value growth, 2013–2015) despite a positive macro backdrop (+6.68% GDP, +31% CCI), signaling category maturity/saturation rather than a shrinking consumer base

**Competition**
- Big's value share fell from 68.4% → 54.4% (Q1'13–Q4'15), while Action's rose from 3.6% → 19.1%, narrowing the gap from ~19x to ~2.8x
- Big lost share in *every* channel and region, with the steepest drop in TT Rural (70.4% → 52.7%), its numeric distribution (store coverage) is shrinking too, especially in rural areas
- Big's average price index sits 6 points above the category average, among the highest of all five brands, while its promotional bundle penetration remains marginal

**Growth opportunities for Action**
- **TT Rural** is the single most important channel (78% of Action's total brand value) and where Action has scaled fastest, with **TT Central Rural** emerging as a newly strategic sub-region (+12.6% avg. growth) alongside the already strong TT South Rural
- **MT Urban** is still small (6% share) but growing fast (12x in 3 years), it is a longer-term, not immediate, opportunity
- **Regular Powder** is Action's most price-competitive SKU (price index 97 vs. category) and fits the rural shopper's efficiency-driven, cost-per-wash mindset
- **Liquid detergent** is a structurally attractive white space: Big is the only brand currently competing in it, and the segment is growing at a 32.8% CAGR (2012–2015)
- Action's large/medium pack growth (800g, 3kg) is currently **promotion-led rather than organic**, a sustainability risk flagged for the recommendation

## Recommendations Summary

| | **Key Strategy (Now)** | **Nice-to-Have (Later)** |
|---|---|---|
| **Place** | TT South & Central Rural | MT Urban |
| **Product** | Regular Powder: efficiency-focused, full pack range (400g–4.5kg) | Premium Liquid: fast & convenient |
| **Price** | Affordable | Premium |
| **Promotion** | KOL + sampling to build trial and loyalty at point-of-sale; TVC/online ads, retailer display & basket-deal programs |

**2016 revenue forecast:** +40.97% total growth, combining +24.05% from the existing portfolio and +16.91% from the new product launch.
